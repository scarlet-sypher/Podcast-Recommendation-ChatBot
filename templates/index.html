About PodcastFinder

PodcastFinder is an AI-powered recommendation engine designed to help you discover podcasts that match your interests. Our intelligent chatbot understands your preferences and suggests podcasts you'll love.

Whether you're looking for true crime stories, educational content, comedy shows, or in-depth interviews, PodcastFinder can help you find your next favorite podcast in seconds.

This project was created using Flask, natural language processing, and a curated database of popular podcasts across various categories.
